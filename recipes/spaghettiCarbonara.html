Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A rich and creamy Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. Perfect for a quick yet indulgent meal.

No cream needed â€” just the silky emulsion of cheese, eggs, and pasta water coating each strand beautifully.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan or Pecorino
  • Salt & freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy. (Add garlic if using, then discard)
  3. Beat eggs with cheese and a generous amount of pepper.
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta, remove from heat.
  5. Stir in egg mixture quickly to avoid scrambling.
  6. Add a splash of pasta water if needed. Serve immediately.
