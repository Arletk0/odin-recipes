Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and vibrant Mediterranean salad full of flavor and color. Ideal as a side dish or a light lunch.

Simple, healthy, and quick to prepare — made with juicy vegetables, briny olives, and tangy feta cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • ½ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 100g feta cheese, crumbled
  • Handful of black olives
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Chop all vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Combine in a bowl with olives and feta.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper. Toss and serve.
